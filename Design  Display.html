Design

The Titanium band is bonded with a new internal aluminium frame through solid‑state.
This is an industry‑first innovation,
using a thermomechanical process that joins these two metals with incredible strength.

New contoured edges and the thinnest borders ever on iPhone make it even more comfortable to hold in your hand.

Display


A Display of True.

Iphone 15 Series Pro has an advanced Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion.
It ramps up refresh rates to 120Hz when you need exceptional graphics performance.
And ramps down to save power when you don’t.

Standby Mode





StandBy3
turns your iPhone into a new,
full-screen experience with glanceable information you can see from a distance.
Just set it on its side while charging to display the time, showcase your favourite photos or keep an eye on your widgets.
Thanks to the Always‑On display, it’s persistent.
But at night, the screen dims so you can sleep easy.
